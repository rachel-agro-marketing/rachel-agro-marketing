About Us

Reliable Piping & Pumping Solutions Since 2000

Rachel Agro Marketing was founded in the year 2000 with a simple goal — to provide industries with durable, efficient, and cost-effective piping and pumping systems. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted industrial solution providers across Madhya Pradesh, serving major clients in the pharmaceutical, chemical, and manufacturing sectors.

From the very beginning, our focus has been on quality products, professional installation, and exceptional customer support. We deal in HDPE, PP, PP-R, and CPVC pipes & fittings, along with a comprehensive range of industrial pumps. Our skilled team handles everything from supply and installation to maintenance — ensuring a smooth, worry-free experience for every client.

Today, with more than 25 years of experience, we are proud to have contributed to the infrastructure and process efficiency of numerous leading industries. Our dedication to innovation, reliability, and long-term partnerships continues to drive our success.

Our Core Values

  • Quality First: We deliver only tested and reliable products.
  • Customer Focus: Tailored solutions that fit industrial needs.
  • Integrity: Honest communication and transparent dealings.
  • Commitment: On-time delivery and dependable service.
