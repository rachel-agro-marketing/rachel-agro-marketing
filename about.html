About Rachel Agro Marketing

Trusted industrial piping & pumping solutions since 2000.

Established in 2000, our company has been a trusted name in providing high-quality industrial piping and pumping solutions across Madhya Pradesh and beyond. We specialize in the supply and installation of HDPE, PP, and PP-R pipes and fittings, as well as CPVC pipes and a wide range of industrial pumps. Our products are widely used in diverse industries, particularly in the pharmaceutical and manufacturing sectors, where reliability and performance are critical.

With over two decades of experience, we have built strong partnerships with leading industries by delivering efficient, durable, and cost-effective piping systems tailored to their specific needs. Our commitment to quality, timely execution, and customer satisfaction has helped us grow from a regional supplier to a reputed industry leader. Whether it’s an industrial plant setup, process line installation, or maintenance support, we provide end-to-end solutions that keep industries running smoothly.
